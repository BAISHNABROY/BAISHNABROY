                                <h2>
                                    ALL ABOUT YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT YOUR CHILD
                                </h2>
                               Parents are always very conscious about their Children . And our Society wants a Healthy Grown Children to build a better Future Parents want to provide all the best for their Children . But today’s Parents are so busy in the Daily Schedules and so they want to find a READY RECKONER platform with all the  NECESSARY INFORMATION , GUIDELINES and PROVISIONS . And the New-age Technology can serve the purposes .    

This will help promoting child development through parental training, among other factors, promotes excellent rates of child development. Another influential factor in children's development is the quality of their care which can be handled through our app.

In this app, the users have to provide the NAME , DATE of BIRTH, GENDER, and AGE of the CHILD BELLOW 18 and the PARENTS NAME, MOBILE NUMBER WITH CITY / LOCATION . According to this we will provide the user about the VACCINATION DETAILS in need and set remainder for that. It will also provide the information about the PHYSICAL AND MENTAL HEALTH PARAMETERS AS PER AGE(Using Software) , required baby food supply(DIETS) , baby needs supply , baby ware supply,(linking online shopping) the surrounding best  PEDIATRICIANS NEARBY for the child and their contact details, best SCHOOL AND TUTORS for proper education of the child in the locality, BABY SITTER available nearby , and even the CREATIVITY AND CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITY PAGE (entertainment) information for the child.

Child-care programs are beneficial for childhood development such as learning capabilities and social skills and we can provide this through our app.

Since the optimal development of children is considered vital to society and it is important to understand the social, cognitive, emotional, and educational development of the children , our app – PERFECT PARENTING will come out to be the best for this development.
